The Idea


Indexcare is a Telehealth Application targeted towards parents/guardians of children or individuals between the ages of 4 and 17 years old. This idea is motivated by the need for an application specifically tailored to provide virtual medical consultations for children for non-emergency health needs for both physical and behavioural health

My Roles

- UX Research
- UX Design
- UI Design
- Prototyping

Problem Statement

Children are prone to common illnesses. This situation keeps many children and their parents/guardians in and out of hospitals from time to time. The process of getting medical consultations are usually stressful, time-wasting and frustrating for parents as well as the children. There is also the risk of getting infected with more serious diseases at the hospital. This lack of quick access to medical help has led to people resorting to self-medicating their children and other unsafe health practices.

Soluton

With this App. I Profer a solution that provides a means for parents/guardians to connect with board certified medical practitioners to get consultations via a video interface as well as a health market place where parents can purchase genuine medicines and other medical supplies.

My Visual Solution

Home page

This is the first screen after Registrations. Here, User can see available doctors, then go ahead to view their profiles. A user can also search for doctors or products to buy. A blogpost section is also featured where user can read health related articles.

Description/Appointment Booking

This a Description page, Doctor’s biography is described here, as well as reviews from previous clients. Two possible actions take place here. A user can either decide to book an appointment, which may be scheduled for a later day or a user can select immediate consultation if they want the consultation to happen in that instant.

Home page

This is the first screen after Registrations. Here, User can see available doctors, then go ahead to view their profiles. A user can also search for doctors or products to buy. A blogpost section is also featured where user can read health related articles.

Health Market

Here, a user can purchase medication or other health supplies, typically medications for children.

Video Call

This is a high resolution video interface where consultations are held. Doctors can easily monitor the patient because the video quality makes the session feel almost like a physical consultation.

Payment

This is the Payment screen where users pay for consultation, This payment can be done via different Payment card options.

DISCOVER PHASE

User Research

The solutions i presented above were made possible with the aid of certain processes. I carried out user research to find out the potential users’ points of view, their frustations and their struggle with getting access to healthcare for their children. I used Qualitative and Quantitative research methods

Quantitative Research

Using Surveys, done with Google forms, I was able to reach an audience of 30 people. Below, is a chart of some important survey questions and the responses i got.









Qualitative Research

For the Qualitative Research , I interviewed a total of 15 participants, including men and women on Zoom video calls. From the responses i got, I was able to deduce the following facts;

Most parents do not take children to the hospital unless they believe it is serious

Self-medication and treatment guessing is usually the first option

Self-medication sometimes goes wrong

Most parents these days, are familiar with virtual solutions

They will not hesitate to get medical help everytime if they had a trustworthy platform

Conclusion

I carried out this project in order to find a solution to the problem of access to health care, also to provide a means where people can buy genuine medical products to complete their treatments and i chose to restrict this product at this time to only cater for paediatric access. Research played a big role in validating the need for this product. I learnt that It is often difficult for parents/guardians to take time off their schedules to go wait long hours at the hospital and it was interesting to see how receptive my interviewees were, about the idea of virtual medical consultation, granted it was not for serious illnesses. Going forward, I believe that with the best business strategies, this product will reach a large number of people and it will be useful in reducing these challenges.





challenges & Lessons

It was a bit challenging for me to understand how to create a visual solution based on the research but with some patience and effort, it all came together eventually. I learnt that the importance of research in creating a product cannot be overemphasized. It gives more clarity to the problem and how to solve it.
